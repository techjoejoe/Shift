The SHIFT Framework™

Master five phases that turn good leaders into exceptional ones

S

Set Context

Define clear goals and expectations from the start

Specific observable behaviors
  • Explain the purpose, impact, and priority.
  • Define success: outcome, quality bar, and date.
  • Clarify boundaries: scope, budget, constraints.
  • Share an example or template.
  • Ask, “What do you need from me?”
H

Hear Fully

Listen deeply to understand all perspectives

Specific observable behaviors
  • Ask open questions; avoid interrupting.
  • Paraphrase to confirm understanding.
  • Name risks and concerns you hear.
  • Invite other viewpoints in the room.
  • Summarize next step you heard.
I

Invite Ownership

Empower teams to lead and take responsibility

Specific observable behaviors
  • Ask for their proposed approach.
  • Confirm the single owner by name.
  • Define decision rights and guardrails.
  • Agree on first milestone and date.
  • Have the owner restate the commitment.
F

Explore Options

Foster creative solutions and innovation

Specific observable behaviors
  • Generate 2-3 viable options.
  • Compare trade offs with simple criteria.
  • Timebox exploration to 5-10 minutes.
  • Pick a path and a next micro step.
  • Capture who does what by when.
T

Track Progress

Monitor results and celebrate achievements

Specific observable behaviors
  • Set update cadence and channel.
  • Define 2-3 milestones with dates.
  • Track in a visible place everyone sees.
  • Review what is working and what is not.
  • Celebrate wins or reset the plan fast.
